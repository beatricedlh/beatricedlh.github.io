Be My Valentine is a catalogue exploring 222 Valentine’s Day postcards from the Newberry Library Collection. The project examines the tension between mass production and personal sentiment, highlighting how standardized printed imagery coexists with intimate handwritten messages, stamps, and marks of circulation.

The publication is accompanied by a selection of reprinted postcards.
