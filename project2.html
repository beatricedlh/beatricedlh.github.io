In the spring of 2023, I created a book focused on communicating the different classifications of typefaces.

Each section features examples of each style, along with a discussion of the formal differences between the categories, their relevance to typographic tasks, and a brief overview of their origins.

  • 7-7-7 (2024)
  • HookedOn (2023)
